Arrived

  • 1 min read

my Role

  • Logo Design

Arrived is an app that offers help to immigrants to the US. As a design intern, I was assigned to redesign the logo of the app.

From ideation to the first draft

I started by evaluating the original logo on my own, then asked random people about their impressions of the logo. The major issues found including:

  • The flags look low-resolution.
  • The meaning of the white flag is confusing, and has a connotation of giving in.
  • The overall feel of the logo doesn't link to the name of the app.
  • The logo is too realistic and doesn't look like a logo for a mobile app.

I asked about the founder’s expectations and came up with some motives related to the word ”Arrived” and immigration: road, bird migration, maze, torch on the Statue of Liberty, mountain-climbing, and the American flag. I developed them into some drafts using a mix of Illustrator, Sketch and Photoshop. People on my team, especially the founder, loved these logos; we’re running a vote to choose a winner. I will continue to refine the logo once a decision is made.
