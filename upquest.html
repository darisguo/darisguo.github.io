UpQuest

This is largely based on a course project from Stanford's CS147: Introduction to HCI design. I served as the designer of the team, working with two engineers.

Problem

K-12 teachers often start the school year with limited information about the knowledge levels of their students. They also find it hard to track studentsâ€™ progress over time.

Solution

Our application allows teachers to give formative assessments to their students and quickly understand their strong and weak points.

constraints

  • The course is centered on mobile app design while the nature of our app is most suitable in the form of a web app.
  • Our target users, initially K-12 teachers and students, then teachers only, are hard to get. Thus we had very limited access to real users.
